Audio description

AD is a voice narration that describes or provides information on the most significant aspects of the image, such as gestures, places, actions, etc.

The purpose of audio description is to provide support for the blind or visually impaired, so the scenes may be fully understood.

How do we do this?

• By analyzing the artwork.
• Creating a script with professional experts in AD, films and series.
• Narrating with talented professionals trained in audio description.
• Mixing Audio and integrating it to the soundtrack.
• Editing voices and voice tones according to the content to enable uniformity in the audio-visual product and its audio description.

Subtitling

We create subtitles for the deaf.
We translate: dialect Castilian Spanish to correct Castilian Spanish / Castilian Spanish to English/English to Castilian Spanish

What is included in this process?

• Transcription of the original script.
• Translation of the subtitles from the template.
• Creating the Spotting List (subtitle directions).
• Editing and mixing on Video (incrustation on images)

Subtitling for people hard of hearing (SPHH)

SPHH converts sound language to text. We translate spoken speech, as well as background noise, sound effects, music and silences, so as to provide a better understanding of the scene.

Delivery Formats

The subtitled material can be exported in múltiple formats:

.txt
.ass
.srt
Open (Embedded on the image)
Closed (Closed Caption)
